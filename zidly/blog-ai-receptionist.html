How AI Receptionists Help Small Businesses Never Miss a Call

Every missed call is a customer walking to your competitor. AI voice agents are changing that.

The Problem: Missed Calls Kill Revenue

62% of callers who reach voicemail hang up without leaving a message. They call the next business on Google. The average small business misses 10-15 calls per week. A plumber missing 5 calls/week at $500 average job value is losing $10,000/month to voicemail.

What Is an AI Receptionist?

An AI receptionist answers phone calls in a natural, conversational voice. Unlike old IVR systems, modern AI receptionists answer questions about services, pricing, and hours, book appointments, capture caller info as leads, and send confirmations automatically.

AI vs Human Receptionist

A human receptionist costs $1,500-$3,000/month, works limited hours, calls in sick, and handles one call at a time. An AI costs a fraction, works 24/7/365, never calls in sick, and handles unlimited simultaneous calls. Plus it manages reviews, follow-ups, and booking.

Three Routing Modes

Set the AI to answer all calls (full replacement), after-hours only, or as backup for calls your team misses. Switch modes from your phone anytime.

Custom Voice and Knowledge

Choose male or female voice, various tones. Program it with as much knowledge as you want — services, pricing, policies, team members, FAQs. Setup takes minutes, not weeks.
